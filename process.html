Our Process

1 / 5
Gather materials to assemble 1 face shield.
2 / 5
Attach foam to band area of face shield frame.
3 / 5
Attach plastic sheet into face shield frame.
4 / 5
Tie hair ties into pre-printed holes of face shield frame.
5 / 5
Attach ear saver through loops of hair ties. Complete!
❮ ❯

Above, you will see our process of assembling the face shields. As we are working on many requests, the process is extremely simple and takes around 2-3 minutes to complete. By also using affordable materials and 3D printing technology, it allows us to keep the production cost of each face shield extremely low.
