Our Goal

We are producing and donating face shields to healthcare and essential workers in our community free of cost in order to protect those who are working on the frontlines everyday to protect us. By using 3D printing, clear plastic sheets, and hair ties, we are creating a low cost and reusable tool that will help save lives.
